Who are we?

Welcome to De Baker's & More, where every bite tells a story of exquisite flavors and passion for baking. Our artisan bakery is a haven for pastry lovers, offering a delightful range of freshly baked goods that will tantalize your taste buds. Step into our cozy bakery and immerse yourself in the warm aroma of bread, pastries, and cakes. From delicious biscuits to decadent chocolate cakes, each creation is crafted with love and attention to detail. Our skilled baker uses the finest ingredients to ensure every bite is a moment of pure bliss. Savor the rich aromas and heavenly textures as you explore our diverse menu. At De Baker's & More, we take pride in creating unforgettable experiences through our baked goods. Whether you're picking up a treat for yourself or celebrating a special occasion, our bakery is a place where memories are made and shared. Come and experience the magic of artisan baking at De Baker's & More.
